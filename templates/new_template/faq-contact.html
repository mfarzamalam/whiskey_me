All Questions

WHAT KIND OF WHISKY WILL I GET?

All our pouches are filled with Single Malt or World Whisky.
We only pick the very best and we never send the same one
twice – and why would we, when there are more than 100 malt
distilleries in Scotland alone to choose from?

HOW DO I CANCEL MY SUBSCRIPTION?

Sign into your Whisky Me account and click on 'Manage
Subscription' to cancel your membership. We’ll be sorry to
see you go but you’re welcome back anytime.

I NEED HELP - CAN I CONTACT YOU DIRECTLY?

Whether you want to send us feedback, need help with
something, or just want to tell us what you think of your
latest dram, you can reach us on our social channels or by
emailing hello@whisky-me.com. Whatever it is, we’ll get back
to you within 48 hours.

I'M NEW TO WHISKY: HOW DO I CHOOSE A
SUBSCRIPTION?

We’ve made the whole process of discovering new whisky as easy as
possible and it starts with choosing the type of
whisky you want: Scotch or World. Not sure how to pick
between the two? We can help with that. Once you’ve chosen,
you’ll need to sign up to either a monthly or annual
subscription, we’ll send you a confirmation email and that’s
it: your Whisky Me adventure is underway

I'VE GOT AN ANNUAL SUBSCRIPTION: WILL IT
AUTOMATICALLY RENEW?

It depends. If you subscribed after 4th May 2021, when our
new website went live, your subscription will automatically
renew (we'll send you an email before this happens). If you
subscribed before 4th May 2021 your subscription wont
automatically renew and you will need to purchase a new
subscription to continue receiving delicious whisky. We will
send you an email when your subscription is close to ending
and you will be able to re-subscribe using your same details
which will ensure that your account keeps a log of all your
past whiskies.

ARE THERE ANY MORE MEMBER REWARDS COMING?

We're working on it...

SOMEONE GAVE ME A GIFT CARD: WHAT DO I DO NOW?

Follow the instructions on the gift email you’ve received
and the first thing we’ll ask you to do is to choose between
Scotch or World Whisky for your subscription. We can help
you pick if you’re not certain which one’s for you. Next
you’ll need to choose the duration of your subscription –
your gift will be for a set number of months but if you want
to extend it, you’ll be able to do that too. And once all
that’s done, we’ll send you a confirmation email – and all
that remains is to wait patiently for your first dram to
arrive.

I WANT TO SEND SOMEONE A GIFT CARD: WHAT DO I DO?

From our gift card page, choose the number of months that
you’d like the subscription to run for, then add the
subscription to your basket. Once you’ve checked out and
paid, we’ll send you an email that you can share with the
lucky recipient – there’ll be a code in that email that they
can quickly and easily redeem. And once they’ve picked the
whisky they want, they’ll start receiving monthly drams
through their letterbox almost immediately (so expect a big
thank you from them sooner rather than later).

MY DAD/GRAN/DOG ISN'T GREAT WITH COMPUTERS: CAN
I BUY ON THEIR BEHALF?

Yes. Choose a type of whisky for them along with the
subscription duration then check out using their details.
The email address you provide will be where we send your
order confirmation and monthly shipment emails. We recommend
using your own email address and their physical address.
(Don't feed dogs whisky).

WHEN WILL MY WHISKY BE SENT?

Scotch Single Malt and World Whisky is sent on the 8th of
each month, unless the 8th falls on a weekend – when that
happens, we ship out on the first Monday after that. Once
you’ve subscribed, we’ll keep you updated on the arrival of
your next whisky by email. The cut-off for new subscriptions
is always the last day of the preceding month, so if you
order on the 1st, 2nd, 3rd or 4th, you’ll wait just a little
more than a month for your first drop.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE FOR MY WHISKY TO ARRIVE?

We ship using Royal Mail 2nd class, so the shipping time is
normally 2-5 working days – but delivery times vary a little
depending on where you live. We’ll email you a shipping
notification when your whisky is out for delivery: if your
whisky hasn’t arrived and it’s been more than 7 working days
since you got that email, let us know.

OK, IT'S BEEN SEVEN WORKING DAYS AFTER THE
DISPATCH EMAIL AND STILL NO WHISKY. WHAT NOW?

Send your subscription details and the name of the dram
you’re waiting on to hello@whisky-me.com and we’ll get
straight on to Royal Mail to find out what’s happening. But
don’t worry: even if the posties have misplaced it, we’ll
happily pop a replacement pouch in the post for you.

BAD NEWS: MY WHISKY TURNED UP DAMAGED

In the really (really) rare event that your whisky arrives
damaged, drop us a line at hello@whisky-me.com with your
subscription details. If you can, send us a picture of the
damage – it helps speed things up if we can see what the
damage is.

HOW DO I UPDATE MY EMAIL OR POSTAL ADDRESS?

Sign into your Whisky Me account and click on 'Manage
Subscription' to update your postal or email address. If
you’re moving home, remember to let us know before the end
of the calendar month, when we process all our deliveries –
otherwise your dram might end up at your old place.

DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?

Not yet, but watch this space. Better yet, follow us on
social and we’ll announce there as soon as we do

DOES THE POUCH AFFECT THE FLAVOUR?

Absolutely not. We have tested our pouches up to the three
years (why you would keep whisky that long is beyond us...)
and found it to be as fresh as the day it was bottled.

CAN I RECYCLE THE POUCH AT HOME?

Chances are your local authority wont collect your pouches
(yet) but there are a growing number of recycling services
where you can drop them off, and these tend to be located at
supermarkets, schools and nurseries. If all else fails,
remember you can send them back to us for free whisky.

CAN I SEND BACK THE OLD STYLE POUCHES?

Of course! We've still got love for our old pouches, and
whilst we won't be refilling old pouches that you send back,
we will make sure they get sent for recycling.
