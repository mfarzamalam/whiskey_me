OUR MISSION TO YOU

WHISKY ME exists to fill more
mouths with the Greatest
whiskies in the world.

We’re founded by Thomas Aske and Tristan
Stephenson of Black Rock – the east
London bar that’s bucked convention and
made whisky accessible to all since
2016

We're not interested in being snobby or
elitist about whisky. We know there's no
such thing as a typical whisky drinker
and we're here to prove it, by dropping
drams of the world's greatest whiskies
through as many letterboxes as possible.



There’s no gatekeeping here, no tweed
jackets or old school ties or any of
that nonsense – just amazing whiskies
heading straight to your door.
Welcome to a better world of whisky.

How we get
things done.

1

The world’s best whiskies.

We curate our monthly selections with some of the
greatest distilleries in the world, so that every new
dram is incredible. And we never send the same dram
twice.

2

A simpler experience.

We’re here to demystify whisky and simplify the experience – so each delivery comes with simple-to-understand tasting notes, pairings, live online tastings and general whisky know-how.

3

Affordable and accessible

We work with suppliers directly to keep WHISKY ME affordable to all – the 6cl dram we send would cost you £16 in a bar, but we deliver it to your door for half that. The Pouch also helps us keep the cost down...

4

Don’t be scared of the pouch

Why don’t we ship our whisky in bottles? Because our pouches are strong, durable, cheaper to ship, easier to deliver, and don’t affect the taste of the whisky. They look cool too, but that’s just a bonus

5

Join the club

You’re not just signing up for whisky through your letterbox – you’ll also join our community of curious drinkers to rate your drams, share recommendations and build your own digital whisky cellar.
